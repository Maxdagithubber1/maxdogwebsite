Dogs

Dogs are domestic mammals of the family Canidae and their scientific name is Canis lupus familiaris. Dogs are a subspecies of the gray wolf and are also related to foxes and jackals. Dogs are one of the two most ubiquitous and most popular domestic animals in the world. The world's dog population is estimated to be around 1 billion.

Dogs are loving companions that you will never regret buying!
